    Electric boiler Ariston Lydos Eco 100 V, 100L, 1800 W

    Ariston Lydos Eco 100V is an electric boiler with a modern design, LED display and multiple functions, specially designed for reliability and comfort in use. The boiler is made of quality materials and benefits from the latest generation technology:
    • WaterPlus technology - keeps cold water in the lower part of the tank, thus reducing its mixing with accumulated hot water; this ensures more available hot water, which allows you to benefit from the comfort of an additional hot shower at any time;
    • Eco Evo function - remembers your daily habits to provide hot water only when you need it, thus managing to save energy;
    • Titanshield technology - the inner surface of the tank is covered with a titanium enamel, an anticorrosive and rust-resistant material; a large magnesium anode increases the natural properties of titanium, extending the life of the water heater;
    • The T-MAX function to deal with unexpected hot water needs;
    • Absolute safety system: a set of functions to maintain the good functioning of the product in the event of a water or electricity supply interruption;
    • Easy-to-use LED display, similar to a smartphone;
    • Ecological polyurethane insulation, to maintain the temperature;
    • Manual and precise temperature setting;
    • Copper heating element, to reduce limescale deposits;
    The Ariston Lydos Eco 100V boiler is installed vertically, has a capacity of 100 liters and is a model that lends itself to being placed in large rooms.

    Electric boiler Bosch Tronic TR2000T 80B 80 L 2000 W

    Bosch Tronic TR2000T 80B is a vertical electric water heater intended for the preparation of domestic hot water. The boiler has a volume of 80 liters and is placed vertically above the sink, with the connections in the lower part. The product has characteristics that attest to both its quality and functionality:
    • High-density thermal insulation, CFC-free, for energy saving and ecological character;
    • Internal enamelled steel tank, for long life;
    • Oval flange, for reliable protection against overpressure;
    • Supports high pressures, up to 8 bar;
    • Protective magnesium anode, for increased protection against corrosion;
    • Simple installation with the universal mounting bracket;
    Due to its dimensions, 440 x 844 x 440 mm, the Bosch Tronic TR2000T 80B water heater can be placed in both small and large rooms.
